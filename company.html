Built to Serve Your Campaign

We are a boutique data analytics firm specializing in actionable political analysis. Our diverse backgrounds merge data expertise with hands-on political campaign experience. We understand the demands of campaigning, along with the core challenges that stand in the way of success.


We know that getting in front of your constituents is just not enough. What matters most is getting your name, face and voice in front of the actual voters. Polidata makes voter engagement data easily accessible to the many campaigns that have neither access to, nor funds available to work with big expensive consultants or data purveyors.

Chris Awram

Founder & CEO

Chris Awram boasts an International Baccalaureate education and graduates this semester from the Beedie School of Business. He balances the seasonal nature of Polidata's demands with his work as a Business Analyst for the federal government.

Previous experience volunteering for municipal and provincial election campaigns gives Chris an appreciation for all the work involved.
