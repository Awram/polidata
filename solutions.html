The Voter Engagement Report

Our flagship product breaks down your riding into neighbourhoods that are each assigned a Voter Engagement Score. This allows you to target your marketing and door-to-door canvassing to the areas that are predicted to have the highest voter turnout. There's nothing wrong with gut instinct and experience, but it should be complemented with data-driven decisions.
